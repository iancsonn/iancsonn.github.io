Ian is a musical Sound Designer and Audio Engineer based in Boston and New York City. He is interested in the cross section of live music, large systems, and commercial theatre. He is known by his peers for stretching his budgets and pushing the boundaries of student organized theatre at Emerson College, where he is pursuing his BFA in Theatre Design/Technology. He is a student member of TSDCA and student/associate member of ASDP.
